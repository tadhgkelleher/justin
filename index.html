About Justin

With over 25 years of experience as a professional drummer, Justin Healy has worked with some of the best artists in the music industry. Known for his versatility and precision, he is available for a range of music projects including studio recording, live performances, and drum lessons.

Career Highlights

Studio Work

Justin has contributed to numerous hit records as a studio drummer, collaborating with top producers and artists across various genres.

Live Performances

He has performed live on major TV shows, including RTE's *The Late Late Show*, and played alongside high-profile bands and artists.
