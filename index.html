About Me

Welcome! I'm Justin Healy, a professional drummer with decades of experience in live performances, studio recording, and music education. Over the years, I've worked with world-renowned bands, appeared on national television, and shared my passion for rhythm as an educator and mentor to aspiring musicians. Whether it's rocking out with iconic groups or shaping the next generation of drummers, music has been my life and soul.

Career Highlights

Television Performances

Regular drummer on RTE's *The Late Late Show* and *Tubridy Tonight*. A frequent presence in Irish television, delivering memorable live performances.

Studio & Live Performances

Played with iconic acts such as The Golden Horde, The Forget Me Nots, and Mik Pyro & the Dublin Blues Cartel. Extensive recording experience with Sony Music and Setanta Records.

Music Education

Founder of Rockskool, drum tutor at the Dublin School of Music, and mentor at Eezee Drumz Music. Passionate about helping students find their rhythm and voice.

Collaborations

Worked with artists like Eamon Dowd & The Racketeers, John Murphy Band, and Hank Halfhead & The Rambling Turkeys. A trusted consultant in studio setups and sound design.
