The Rhythm Behind Iconic Sounds

Justin Healy is a master of his craft, recognized as one of the most influential drummers in live and studio music. His impeccable timing, creative precision, and dynamic energy have set a new standard in the music industry. Whether performing on the biggest stages or recording chart-topping hits, Justin's drumming leaves an indelible mark.

Career Highlights

With a career spanning decades, Justin has collaborated with legendary acts, performed on national television, and inspired countless musicians.

Television Appearances: A regular presence on RTE's The Late Late Show and Tubridy Tonight, delivering powerful live performances.

Collaborations: Played with renowned groups like The Golden Horde, Forget Me Nots, and Mik Pyro & the Dublin Blues Cartel.
