About Justin

Justin Healy is a versatile drummer and music educator, known for his expertise in studio production and educational roles. With years of experience, he has worked with a variety of artists and bands across genres. He also specializes in drum education, teaching students from all walks of life and guiding them to reach their fullest potential in music.

Services

Justin offers a range of services to elevate your musical projects:

Studio Production

Justin offers hands-on guidance through every stage of production, from pre-production to mastering. Whether you're recording your first track or a full album, Justin's expertise ensures a polished and professional sound.

Creative Consulting

Drawing on his extensive performance experience, Justin offers insights into music production, artist development, and project direction. His guidance ensures that each project stays true to the artist’s vision while achieving commercial success.

Project Direction

Justin’s project direction services cater to artists and bands looking to elevate their creative output. With a deep understanding of the music industry, he helps clients navigate the creative and business aspects of music production.
